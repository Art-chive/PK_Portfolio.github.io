ABOUT US


Welcome to our creative space!


We are Kim Elquiero and Paolo Nicolas, a second-year students currently taking Bachelor of Science in Entertainment and Multimedia Computing, major in Digital Animation at Bicol State College of Applied Sciences and Technology. Our journey in the vibrant world of digital animation has been fueled by our unwavering resilience and dedication. As aspiring animators, we constantly strive to push the boundaries of our creativity and technical skills, embracing every challenge as an opportunity to learn and grow. We believe that hard work and perseverance are the keys to achieving excellence in our craft.

Creative Process

  • Kim Elquiero
    Kim's artistic style features vibrant colors, bold contrasts, and energetic compositions.
    They often explore fantasy and adventure themes, creating imaginative worlds with playful yet meticulous detail.
  • Paolo Nicolas
    Paolo's style leans towards a more realistic and nuanced palette, focusing on the subtleties of light and shadow to create depth and emotion. His animations often center around human experiences and nature, conveying stories with a gentle, contemplative touch.
  • Kim Elquiero
    Kim excels in camera angles, cinematography, and artistic ideas, bringing a unique perspective and visual storytelling to their animations. Their proficiency in framing scenes and creating dynamic compositions enhances the narrative impact of their work.
  • Paolo Nicolas
    Paolo specializes in character design and has an exceptional attention to intricate details. His ability to create lifelike and expressive characters, combined with his skill in detailed texturing and modeling, brings a strong sense of realism and emotion to his animations.
  • Kim Elquiero
    Kim's creative process is spontaneous and iterative, often starting with rough sketches and broad concepts before refining the details. They thrive in collaborative settings, drawing inspiration from team brainstorming sessions and feedback to enhance their projects.
  • Paolo Nicolas
    Paolo approaches his work with meticulous planning and structure, beginning with thorough research and detailed storyboards. He values a methodical workflow, ensuring each step is carefully executed and aligned with the overall vision of the project.
